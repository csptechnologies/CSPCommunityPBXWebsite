CSP Community PBX

Welcome to CSP Community PBX! We are a Community PBX where Telephone Hobbyists can get hosted lines for over 400+ types of phones! Some Phones types we support include Cisco Enterprise (78XX, 88XX, and CIPC), Cisco 3PCC/SPA, Mitel, Polycom, Grandstream, Yealink, Fanvil, Gigaset, and So much more!
